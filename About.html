About ICAISA-2026

ICAISA 2026 (International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Smart Applications) is a global platform dedicated to advancing research and innovation in artificial intelligence and intelligent technologies. It brings together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to explore emerging trends, share knowledge, and discuss the latest developments shaping the future of AI. With rapid progress in machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and data analytics, the conference focuses on how these technologies are transforming industries and enabling the development of smart solutions across domains such as healthcare, smart cities, cybersecurity, and automation.

The conference aims to foster global collaboration and meaningful knowledge exchange by providing a space for presenting research findings, sharing innovative ideas, and discussing advancements in AI-driven systems. It emphasizes bridging the gap between theoretical research and real-world implementation, encouraging interdisciplinary discussions that address practical challenges in modern technological ecosystems. By connecting experts from academia and industry, ICAISA 2026 supports the development of intelligent applications and promotes collaborative efforts to solve real-world problems through the effective use of artificial intelligence.

ICAISA 2026 features a comprehensive program that includes keynote lectures by distinguished speakers, technical paper presentations, panel discussions, and interactive workshops. These sessions provide valuable insights into both emerging research areas and practical applications of AI technologies. With a strong focus on innovation, interdisciplinary collaboration, and real-world impact, the conference aims to accelerate the development of sustainable, efficient, and intelligent systems. It also serves as a dynamic platform for networking and professional growth, helping participants build connections and contribute to the advancement of future-ready digital ecosystems.
