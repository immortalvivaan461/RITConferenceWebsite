About the Conference

ICAISA-2026 invites original research papers from academicians, researchers, and industry professionals in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Smart Applications. The conference aims to provide a global platform for presenting innovative ideas, exchanging knowledge, and fostering collaboration.

Authors are encouraged to submit high-quality, unpublished research that addresses emerging challenges and advancements in AI, Machine Learning, Smart Systems, and Sustainable Technologies.

Submission Guidelines

Authors must follow the guidelines below while preparing and submitting their manuscripts.

📄

Submit original and unpublished research work only.

📏

Manuscript must follow the conference template format.

🔍

All papers will undergo a peer-review process.

📊

Ensure clear methodology, results, and discussion.

📝

Maximum length: 500-530 pages including references.

🚫

Avoid plagiarism; similarity should be within acceptable limits.

Publication Details

All accepted and presented papers will be published in the official conference proceedings with an ISBN number.

Selected high-quality papers will be recommended for publication in Scopus-indexed / reputed journals (subject to journal review process).
